# Old Connected Printer

This bit of code connect a Mannesman Tally MT85 (yes, for 1985!!) to the cloud via Tramontana  and Arduino.

## Wiring the LPT connector to the Arduino pins

* 1	brown		STROBE		37
* 2	red			D0			53
* 3	orange		D1			51
* 4	pink		D2			49
* 5	yellow		D3			47
* 6	dark green	D4			45
* 7	light green	D5			43
* 8	blue		D6			39
* 9	gray/blue	D7			41
* 10	purple		ACK
* 11	gray		BUSY
* 19	pink/back	GND			GND
